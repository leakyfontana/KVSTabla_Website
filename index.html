KVS Tabla

Sid Komaragiri is a classically trained Tabla player who represents the Farrukhabad Gharana of Tabla. He has been playing Tabla since 2007 and has been learning from Pt. Samir Chatterjee since 2012. Currently he is exploring various ways to incorporate Tabla into Western music genres such as hip/hop, Rap, EDM, and Jazz.
